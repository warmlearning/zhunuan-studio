品牌故事 Our Story

知暖，從一個問題開始 Zhunuan Began With a Question

「為什麼那麼多學生學了多年英語，卻還是無法開口說、看不懂文章？」這個問題，是知暖學習工作室成立的起點。 "Why do so many students study English for years and still can't speak or understand it?" This question was the seed that grew into Zhunuan.

創辦人 Penny 老師在多年的英語教學經驗中，發現傳統填鴨式教學讓學生失去了學習的熱情。於是，她決定打造一個不一樣的學習空間——溫暖的、有方向的、以學生為主體的。 Founder Penny, through years of teaching experience, discovered that traditional rote learning kills students' enthusiasm. She set out to create a different kind of learning space — warm, purposeful, and student-centered.

「知暖」兩字，取自「知足上進、溫暖而堅定」——我們希望每一位學習者都能在這裡找到方向，用最適合自己的方式，穩定地成長。 "Zhunuan" comes from "grounded, warm, and steadfast" — we want every learner to find direction here and grow steadily in their own way.

我們的使命 Our Mission

透過溫暖且有系統的學習方式，以英語學習為起點，打造每個人專屬的無痛學習法，受用終身。以互動、引導、陪伴取代單向教學，養成學生自律與自學能力，讓學習不再是壓力，而是一輩子的夥伴。 Through warm and systematic learning, starting from English, we craft personalized pain-free learning methods that serve learners for life. By replacing one-way instruction with interaction, guidance, and companionship, we cultivate self-discipline and independent learning so that education becomes not a burden, but a lifelong companion.

核心價值 Core Values

我們所相信的 What We Stand For

四個核心價值，引導我們與每一位學習者同行。 Four core values guide us alongside every learner we work with.

溫暖陪伴 Warm Companionship

每一位學員都不是孤單的。我們與您同行，在每一個學習的低潮與高峰旁守候。 No learner is alone. We walk beside you through every high and low in your learning journey.

系統學習 Systematic Learning

有架構、有步驟的學習路徑，讓進步清晰可見，不再感到茫然無措。 Structured, step-by-step learning paths make progress visible and remove the feeling of being lost.

互動引導 Interactive Guidance

用提問、對話、討論取代填鴨，激發學生的主動思考與表達能力。 Replace rote memorization with questions, dialogue, and discussion to spark active thinking.

終身受用 Skills for Life

我們的目標不只是幫您通過一場考試，而是培養帶得走的語言力與學習力。 Our goal is not just passing an exam, but building language and learning skills that last forever.

適合對象 Who We Serve

我們為誰而來 We're Here For You If...

成人英語學習者 Adult English Learners

無論您是希望提升職場英語、準備出國旅遊，還是只是想找回學語言的樂趣，我們都能陪您重新出發。 Whether you want to improve workplace English, prepare for travel, or rediscover the joy of language learning — we're here to restart with you.

升學考生 Exam-Bound Students

面對學測、指考、英檢等重要考試，我們提供有系統的備考輔導，幫助您在壓力中穩定發揮。 Facing GSAT, AST, GEPT or other major exams? We provide systematic prep coaching to help you perform with confidence under pressure.
