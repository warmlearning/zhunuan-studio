小班制課程Small Group Classes

線上 / 線下皆可 Online / In-Person

小班制（3–6人）設計，讓學員在互動與討論中互相激勵，同時又能獲得老師的個別關注。課程以主題式討論、角色扮演、情境練習為主，讓英語在真實交流中自然習得。 Designed for 3–6 students, small group classes combine peer motivation with individual teacher attention. Lessons use thematic discussions, role-plays, and situational practice to acquire English naturally through real interaction.

升學英語輔導Exam Preparation

升學考生專屬 For Students

專為學測、指考、英檢（GEPT）等考試設計的備考課程。老師依照各考試的題型與評分標準，系統性地強化閱讀、寫作、聽力、口語等各項能力，並定期模擬考試，讓您在正式上場時胸有成竹。 Purpose-built preparation for GSAT, AST, GEPT, and other exams. Systematically strengthen reading, writing, listening, and speaking based on each exam's format and rubric, with regular mock tests to build exam-day confidence.

學測 / 指考 / 英檢GSAT / AST / GEPT 模擬考試練習Mock Exams 分數系統提升Score-Focused

成人英語會話Adult English Conversation

成人學習者 For Adults

以真實生活場景為核心，課程內容涵蓋日常會話、國際議題討論、TED 影片賞析、電影影集主題探討等。不只是練習說英語，更是透過有深度的內容，培養用英語思考、表達的能力。 Rooted in real-life scenarios, covering everyday conversation, global issues, TED talks, and movies/TV shows. Beyond just speaking practice — building the ability to truly think and express in English.

TED / 電影主題TED / Movie Themes 國際議題討論Global Topics 口語表達訓練Speaking Focus

學習方法輔導Study Method Coaching

所有學習者 For Everyone

許多學生學不好，不是因為不夠聰明，而是因為還沒找到適合自己的學習方法。我們提供一對一的學習方法診斷，引導您找到方向、設定目標，陪伴您一步一步達成，養成終身受用的自學能力。 Many students struggle not because they lack intelligence, but because they haven't found the right method. We offer one-on-one learning diagnostics to find your direction, set goals, and accompany you step by step toward self-directed lifelong learning.
