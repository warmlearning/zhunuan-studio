常見問題 FAQ

您可能想知道的 You Might Want to Know

Q 課程費用是多少？ How much do the courses cost?

課程費用依類型和需求而定。請直接聯繫我們，老師會根據您的需求提供最適合的方案與報價。 Pricing varies by course type and needs. Contact us directly and Teacher Penny will provide the best plan and quote for you.

Q 可以先試上一堂課嗎？ Can I try a trial class first?

我們提供免費的初次諮詢，讓老師了解您的程度與需求，並為您規劃課程方向。歡迎先聯繫我們安排！ We offer a free initial consultation where Teacher Penny assesses your level and needs to plan your learning direction. Contact us to schedule one!

Q 線上課程用什麼平台上課？ What platform is used for online classes?

主要使用 Google Meet 或 Zoom，依學員偏好彈性調整。只需要一台有鏡頭和麥克風的電腦或手機即可。 We primarily use Google Meet or Zoom, flexible to your preference. You just need a computer or phone with a camera and microphone.

Q 英語程度很差也可以報名嗎？ Can I enroll even if my English is very basic?

當然！知暖歡迎所有程度的學習者。我們會從您現在的起點出發，用最適合您的方式幫助您進步。 Absolutely! Zhunuan welcomes learners of all levels. We start from exactly where you are and help you progress in the way that works best for you.
