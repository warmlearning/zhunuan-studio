創辦人 Founder

Penny 老師 Teacher Penny

創辦人 · 英語教學專家 · 學習引導師 Founder · English Teaching Expert · Learning Coach

Penny 老師擁有豐富的英語教學經驗，擅長以溫暖而有系統的方式引導學生學習。她深信每一位學習者都有無限潛能，只是需要找到屬於自己的方法。 Teacher Penny brings rich English teaching experience and excels at guiding students with warmth and structure. She firmly believes every learner has unlimited potential — they just need to find their own method.

在成立知暖學習工作室之前，Penny 老師已服務過數十位不同年齡、程度與需求的學員，累積了豐富的個人化教學經驗。她特別擅長目標設定與學習方法引導，幫助許多原本對英語失去信心的學生重新找回學習的熱情。 Before founding Zhunuan, Teacher Penny served dozens of students across different ages, levels, and needs, building deep expertise in personalized instruction. She specializes in goal-setting and learning method coaching, helping many students who had lost confidence in English rediscover their passion for learning.

執行助理 Operations

Henry

執行助理 · 行政與營運支援 Executive Assistant · Administration & Operations

Henry 負責知暖學習工作室的行政事務與日常營運，確保每一位學員都能獲得順暢、舒適的學習體驗。從課程安排、溝通協調到品牌推廣，Henry 都全力支援。 Henry handles administrative affairs and daily operations at Zhunuan, ensuring every student enjoys a smooth and comfortable learning experience — from scheduling and coordination to brand promotion.

他相信一個好的學習環境不只需要優秀的老師，也需要完善的後勤支援。正是這樣的信念，讓他全心投入知暖每一個細節的打磨。 He believes a great learning environment requires not just excellent teaching but also strong back-office support. This belief drives his wholehearted attention to every detail at Zhunuan.

我們的文化 Our Culture

知暖的每一天 Every Day at Zhunuan

小而精、溫而堅，這是知暖團隊的日常樣貌。 Small but focused, warm yet steadfast — this is the everyday spirit of the Zhunuan team.

以學員為中心 Student-Centered

每一個決定，都以「對學員最好」為優先考量。 Every decision prioritizes what's best for our students.

持續成長 Continuous Growth

我們自己也是學習者，不斷進修、反思，讓服務品質持續提升。 We're learners too — always studying, reflecting, and improving our service quality.

真誠溝通 Genuine Communication

與學員保持開放、誠實的溝通，讓每段學習關係都建立在信任上。 Open and honest communication with students, building every learning relationship on trust.
